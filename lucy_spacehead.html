The Band.

Lucy Spacehead was a 5 piece punk rock outfit from Philadelphia, PA. The band consisted of Ryan Breen (frontman, guitars, vocals), Calvin Morrison (bass), Matt King (drums), Robbie Baranov (guitar, backing vocals) and Ho Jun (guitar).

The incarnation of Lucy Spacehead was strictly a live band for Ryan Breen, but that didn't make it any less awesome
